KycLogin acts as a silent third-party between two parties, ensuring the secure and seamless execution of high-value transactions. HOW IT WORKS - Funds are held in one of our cold wallets. - Once the agreed conditions are met, funds are released to the designated party. - If the deal isn't respected, the process is stopped and we judge the deal. We do not ask for names. No KYC. Conversations are encrypted and self-destruct after the deal is closed. ENGAGEMENT RULES - You must clearly define the deal terms/conditions (we are not responsible if not). - The seller must deliver exactly what was described. - If the delivery matches the buyer's original description, funds are released. - If the buyer fails to define any conditions, any full delivery is considered valid by default. - We do not verify asset quality, content or legitimacy. - We do not touch accounts or participate in delivery. Our role is strictly to hold and release funds. - We'll not cover crypto transaction network fees when sending and receiving the funds. FEES STRUCTURE (MINIMUM $50,000) Whitehat transactions: - $50,000 to $1M: 1% - Over $1M: 0.5% Blackhat transactions: - $50,000 to $1M: 3% - Over $1M: 1.5% or flat fee (adjusted to risk) Crypto network fees are always paid by the sender. SECURE CHANNELS - For blackhat or sensitive transactions, we switch to Session or SimpleX. - If a transaction is falsely declared whitehat but is later identified as blackhat, we hold no responsibility. DISPUTE HANDLING If any party is found to be acting in bad faith, attempting a pullback or scam: - We reserve full discretion to decide where the funds go. - This may result in full release to one party, a 50/50 refund split or a permanent hold. - By using this service, you agree to accept our final decision.
